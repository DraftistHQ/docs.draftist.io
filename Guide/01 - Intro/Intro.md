[Draft42](https://draft42.io) is a service for publishing personal websites using knowledge base apps as content sources. You write and organize content in your favorite app — we turn it into a live site. ^9f479a

Currently, Draft42 works with [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md). You write in Obsidian, the Draft42 plugin syncs your content, and you manage the site from a web dashboard — domains, appearance, and publishing. ^c3e79e

## Who it's for ^74c886

Anyone who wants to build a content-driven site — bloggers, developers, writers. If you already keep your thoughts in Obsidian, Draft42 lets you publish them straight from your vault — no copying content between apps, no manual formatting, no export/import dance. ^60cdf3

## How it works ^cdecb1

1. **Write** in Obsidian — blog posts, documentation, book, whatever fits your site. ^a60f55
2. **Preview** to see how it looks before going public. ^521259
3. **Publish** to make it live. ^1f545e

Your site is fully customizable — fonts, colors, logos, custom domains — all managed through the [site configurator](Configurator.md). ^02741b

## Getting help ^0087c0

Have a question, found a bug, or want to request a feature? Join us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/nZhbzzbT5A) — it's the right place to get help and share feedback. ^3cd1be
