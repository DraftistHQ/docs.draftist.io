Modules are the building blocks of a [site](Sites.md). Each module adds a different content structure — you pick the ones that fit what you're publishing. ^a3c7d2

## Available modules ^b8e1c5

- **Blog** — a date-sorted feed for articles, updates, and announcements. Posts have statuses, tags, cover images, and descriptions. See [Blog](Blog.md) in the Obsidian section for writing and managing posts. ^c4d9a6
- **Docs** — hierarchical pages organized into sections, ideal for guides, references, books, and any structured documentation. See [Docs](Docs.md) in the Obsidian section for details. ^d7f2b3

## Multiple modules ^f6b3d9

> [!WARNING]
> Currently, in private beta.
^873422

A site can have multiple modules, each with its own name and URL [slug](Slugs.md). For example, a Docs module at `/docs` and a Blog module at `/blog`. Module names and slugs are configured in your site settings. ^1c8e4a

If you rename a module, old URLs redirect to the new slug automatically. ^2d9f5b
