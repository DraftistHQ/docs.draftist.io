The Logo section controls the main logo displayed in your site's header. You can use either text or an image. ^d019b6

## Text logo ^e083a3

By default, your site name is used as a text logo. You can switch to custom text if you want something different, and adjust the font size. ^1b4515

## Image logo ^b21e57

Upload an image to replace the text logo. If your site uses switchable light/dark colors, you can provide separate images for each mode — or a single universal image that works for both. ^9fbe05

Image logos have additional controls: ^e5d96f

- **Scale** — adjusts the logo size ^df8d16
- **Horizontal placement** — shifts the logo left or right ^ceae52
- **Vertical placement** — shifts the logo up or down ^88ab56
