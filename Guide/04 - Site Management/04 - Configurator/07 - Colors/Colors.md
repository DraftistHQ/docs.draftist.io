The Colors section controls your site's color scheme and light/dark mode behavior. ^3d03c3

## Color mode ^1ee5ec

Two modes are available: ^497807

- **Switchable** — visitors can toggle between light and dark themes. You pick a built-in scheme for each. ^143bc7
- **Non-switchable** — your site uses a single fixed color mode. ^00afbb

> [!info]
> Currently, only one built-in theme is available. More themes will be added in future updates. However, you can configure each color via color overrides.
^0efbf9

## Color overrides ^ef3c16

On top of the built-in scheme, you can override individual colors — primary, secondary, text, and more. Overridden colors are marked so you can tell what's customized at a glance, and each override can be reset back to the scheme default. ^c96be0
