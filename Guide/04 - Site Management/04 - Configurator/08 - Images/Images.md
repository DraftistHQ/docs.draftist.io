The Images section controls what visitors see while images are loading. ^98dd95

Three placeholder options are available: ^99c2fe

- **None** — a static solid color placeholder with an icon. ^d1c4e7
- **Blur** — a blurred preview generated from the image. A good compromise between data size and aesthetics. ^7dbf0f
- **Art** — beautiful geometric placeholders derived from the image. Slightly larger in data size, but if aesthetics is a priority, this is a solid choice. Otherwise, one of the options above may be a better fit. ^bb6c8f
