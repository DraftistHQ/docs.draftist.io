The dashboard is your site's home page in the management panel. It shows a quick overview: current status, content provider, domain, and a shortcut to the configurator. ^b89337

## Site Status ^1b672f

The most important control here is the online/offline switch. It determines whether your site is publicly accessible. ^f11c24

- **Online** — your site is live and visible to everyone at its address. ^5d5eae
- **Offline** — public visitors can't access your site. You can still preview and edit it via the draft domain and the configurator. ^556094
